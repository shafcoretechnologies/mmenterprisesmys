Our Story

Four Decades of Serving Karnataka's Schools

Founded in 1985 by Mr. M. Malick, MM Enterprises started as a humble supplier of school accessories in Mysore. What began as a small operation has grown into one of Karnataka's most trusted names in school uniform accessories.

Today, from our store on Abdul Rahman Road, Udaygiri, Mysore, we serve hundreds of schools across the region — supplying everything from belts and socks to medals and custom labels — with the same commitment to quality and service that built our reputation over four decades.

  • GST-registered, government-verified business
  • Wholesale and retail — small and large orders both welcome
  • Custom branding — school name, logo and emblem printing
